About this campaign

This website helps citizens contact their elected officials, sign a petition, and access verifiable sources. It is intended for lawful civic advocacy. It does not perform targeted persuasion by demographic group.

Key facts (short)

  • 2016 MOU: ~$38B in military assistance over 10 years (≈$3.8B/year) — see official sources.
  • ForeignAssistance.gov publishes up-to-date country totals for Israel.
  • Supplemental wartime spending has added billions beyond the MOU in recent years.
