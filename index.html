About this campaign

This website provides verified sources, a prewritten message you can edit, and an easy petition form so concerned citizens can contact their lawmakers and make their voices heard peacefully and lawfully.

Key facts

  • U.S. and Israeli governments signed a 10-year Memorandum of Understanding covering FY2019–FY2028 that pledges ~$38 billion in military assistance (≈ $3.8B/year). (sources below)
  • ForeignAssistance.gov publishes up-to-date country assistance totals for Israel. Use official data when asking Congress to review spending. (source below)
  • Independent analyses show additional supplemental and wartime spending beyond MOU amounts (public reporting available). (source below)
Sources:
  • Council on Foreign Relations — US aid to Israel
  • ForeignAssistance.gov — Israel
  • Congressional Research / MOU citations
